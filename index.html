MAZESTUFF

This is my Portfolio website,
a work in progress.
by Miranda Shugars.

  • Tell Me More

This is the ideal— the website, permanently -under construction- , will only gradually accumulate the requisite expansiveness.


This website, built to display my interests, thoughts, and projects,
is a spatial experiment, explorably winding and internal like Wonderland. Like a culture
it will spread, mutate, off-shoot; you’ll find yourself at blind ends or tripping wildly
into deep recesses. Mapless, each trip to this site forges a unique path.

“A happy-weary walk through the darkening

booths to the late bus which waited, breathing. A sense of membership because of the fitting of the spine to the calculated average of the bus seats. A shared doze, a shared smile, at sixty miles and hour through the flickering night, and at last the familiar depot on the familiar street, echoing and empty but my street in my town.”
—Theodore Sturgeon,

More Than Human
